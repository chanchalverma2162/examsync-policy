# Privacy Policy for ExamSync **Last updated:** July 16, 2026 This Privacy Policy explains how ExamSync ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use the app. We built ExamSync to work entirely on your device, and we take your privacy seriously. ## 1. Summary **ExamSync does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.** All information you enter — exam details, syllabus progress, credentials, roll numbers, admit cards, journal entries, to-do lists, and study activity — is saved locally on your own device only. We do not operate any backend server that receives this data, and the app does not require you to create an account or sign in. ## 2. Information You Store in the App ExamSync lets you keep track of your exam preparation, including: - Exam names, dates, and stages you are tracking - Syllabus subjects, topics, and completion progress - Login emails and passwords for exam portals (stored for your own reference) - Roll numbers and admit card details/images - Exam paper patterns and notes - Daily study activity, streaks, and completed topics - To-do list items - Personal journal entries - Pomodoro/focus session settings and history This information is entered by you and is stored exclusively in local storage on your device (e.g., app-private storage or files you choose to export). It is never uploaded to us, sold, rented, or shared with third parties, because we do not have any server infrastructure to receive it. ## 3. Data Storage and Security - All data is stored locally in the app's private storage on your device. - The app does not transmit your data over the internet during normal use. - Because your data never leaves your device, we — the developers — cannot access, view, or recover it. - Local storage is protected by your device's own operating system security (such as your phone's lock screen, encryption, and app sandboxing). We recommend using a strong device lock screen and keeping your operating system updated, since no method of storage is completely immune to risk if a device itself is lost, stolen, or compromised. - Sensitive fields such as saved passwords and admit cards are stored locally like the rest of your data; please treat your device accordingly, especially if you store exam-portal credentials. ## 4. Import and Export ExamSync allows you to import and export your syllabus and other app data as local files (for example, to back up your data or move it to another device). These files are created and read directly on your device or through your device's file system/share options. We do not receive a copy of any file you export or import. ## 5. Permissions the App May Request Depending on the features you use, the app may request the following device permissions: - **Notifications** — to remind you of upcoming exams, deadlines, and daily study streaks. - **Storage / files** — to let you import or export your syllabus and data, and to attach or view admit card images. - **Camera / photo library** (if applicable) — to let you capture or attach admit card or document images. These permissions are used solely to provide the relevant in-app feature and are not used to collect or transmit data off your device. ## 6. Third-Party Services ExamSync does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs, and does not share data with third parties. > **Note:** If a future version of ExamSync adds a feature that uses an online/AI service (for example, to help generate a syllabus from an uploaded document), that specific feature would send only the content you choose to submit to that feature to a third-party processor to perform the request, and this policy will be updated to disclose that clearly before such a feature is enabled. As of this version, no such transmission occurs. ## 7. Children's Privacy ExamSync does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect information at all — all data stays on the user's own device. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the app, no data has been sent to us for us to remove, as none is ever received by us. ## 8. Your Control Over Your Data Because all data lives on your device, you are always in control of it: - You can edit or delete any entry within the app at any time. - You can export your data for your own backup. - Uninstalling the app removes its locally stored data from your device (subject to your device's own backup systems, such as OS-level device backups, which are controlled by your device manufacturer/OS, not by us). ## 9. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example to reflect new features. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
